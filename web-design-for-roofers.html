Professional Websites for Roofing Companies

Your roofing business deserves a website that works as hard as your crew. We build fast, mobile-first sites that rank in local search and turn visitors into booked jobs.

Why Your Roofing Company Needs a Real Website

When a homeowner discovers a leak or hail damage, the first thing they do is grab their phone and search "roofer near me." If your business doesn't show up — or if your site looks like it was built in 2009 — that lead goes straight to your competitor. It's that simple.

A Facebook page or a listing on Angi isn't enough anymore. Those platforms control your visibility, your reviews, and your leads. A website you own is the foundation of your online presence. It's where Google sends traffic, where homeowners check your credibility, and where storm-damage leads convert into signed contracts.

Most roofing companies we talk to are running on word-of-mouth and referrals alone. That works — until it doesn't. A professional website gives you a second lead channel that runs 24/7, even when your phone is off and your crew is on a roof.

What Every Roofing Website Needs to Convert

A good-looking site isn't enough. Your website needs to be built for the way homeowners actually hire roofers — fast decisions, trust signals, and easy contact options. Here's what we include in every roofing site we build:

Click-to-Call on Mobile
One tap and the homeowner is talking to you. No digging through menus or copying numbers.
Emergency Service Banner
A persistent, attention-grabbing banner for storm damage and emergency repairs that drives urgent calls.
Before & After Gallery
Nothing sells roofing work like visual proof. Showcase your best jobs with side-by-side photo comparisons.
Service Area Map
Show homeowners exactly which neighborhoods, cities, and counties you cover — great for local SEO too.
Google Reviews Integration
Pull in your best Google reviews automatically so visitors see social proof without leaving your site.
Trust Badges & Licensing
Display your license number, insurance status, manufacturer certifications, and BBB rating front and center.

Every feature is built with one goal: making it as easy as possible for a homeowner to pick up the phone or fill out your contact form. We don't add fluff — we add features that generate leads.
