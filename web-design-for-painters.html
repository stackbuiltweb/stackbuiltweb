PAINTING WEBSITES

Professional Websites for Painting Companies

Painting is a visual trade — your website should prove it. We build portfolio-driven sites that showcase your best work, rank in local search, and turn browsers into booked estimates.

Why Your Painting Business Needs a Website That Works

Homeowners choosing a painter do something different than homeowners choosing a plumber: they browse. They look at photos, compare styles, and imagine what their own home could look like. If your online presence is a Facebook page with a few blurry job site photos, you're losing to the painter with a clean portfolio and a professional website.

The painting industry is competitive, and the companies growing the fastest are the ones showing up in "painter near me" searches with a site that immediately builds trust. We're talking sharp before-and-after photos, clear service descriptions, and a quote request form that takes 30 seconds to fill out.

A website isn't just a digital business card. It's your best salesperson — one that works around the clock, never takes a day off, and shows your work to every potential customer in your service area. We build sites that do exactly that.

Features That Win Painting Jobs Online

Your website needs to do two things: show off your craftsmanship and make it effortless for homeowners to reach you. Here's what we include in every painting website:

Before & After Portfolio Gallery
The single most powerful feature on any painter's website. Side-by-side comparisons of your work that sell the job before you ever show up for the estimate.
Color Consultation Booking
Let homeowners book a consultation directly from your site. This pre-qualifies leads and sets you apart from competitors who just list a phone number.
Neighborhood-Focused SEO
Dedicated content targeting specific neighborhoods and subdivisions where you've done work. "We painted 12 homes in Viera this year" hits different than a generic service page.
Review & Testimonial Section
Pull in your Google reviews and feature written testimonials from happy homeowners. Social proof is everything in the painting business.
Room Visualizer Potential
We can integrate color preview tools or link to manufacturer visualizers so homeowners can preview paint colors on their own walls.
Service Breakdown Pages
Separate pages for interior painting, exterior painting, cabinet refinishing, and commercial work — each optimized for the searches that matter.

Painting is one of those trades where the work speaks for itself. We build your website to let it do exactly that — beautifully, on every device, loading in under two seconds.
