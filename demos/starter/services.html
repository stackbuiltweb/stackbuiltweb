What We Do

Our painting services.

From interior walls to exterior facades, cabinets to decks. Every project gets the True Coat treatment.

01

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, trim, and accent walls. We use premium paints and take meticulous care of your furniture and floors. Every room is taped, draped, and protected before we start.

02

Exterior Painting

Full exterior preparation including power washing, scraping, and priming before two coats of weather-resistant paint. We protect your landscaping and clean up every day.

03

Cabinet Refinishing

Transform your kitchen without a full remodel. Sand, prime, paint, and seal for a factory-smooth finish. Saves thousands compared to full replacement.

04

Deck & Fence Staining

Protect and beautify your outdoor wood with professional-grade stains and sealants. We clean, sand, and apply multiple coats for lasting protection.

05

Wallpaper Removal

Clean removal without damaging your walls. Includes surface prep for painting — skim coating, sanding, and priming so your new color goes on smooth.

06

Color Consultation

Not sure what colors to choose? Our team helps you select the perfect palette for your space. We bring samples to your home so you can see them in your own lighting.
