About

Meet Sofia.

I have been behind a lens since I was fourteen. After studying photojournalism at UCF and shooting for publications across Florida, I opened Golden Coast Studios in 2019. Every session I shoot is built around one idea: real moments, real light, real people.

I do not do over-posed or over-edited. I believe the best photos happen when you forget the camera is there. That philosophy has taken me from editorial assignments to weddings, corporate events, and branding sessions across Brevard County and beyond.

Published in Florida Today, Space Coast Living, and Orlando Magazine. UCF School of Journalism alumna. CRM-integrated booking and a custom analytics dashboard track every client from inquiry to delivery.

FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before booking. Still have questions? Just reach out.

For portraits and headshots, 2-3 weeks is ideal. Weddings should be booked 6-12 months in advance. I only take a limited number of weddings per month to ensure every couple gets my full attention.
I am based in Cocoa Beach and regularly shoot across Brevard County. For destination weddings and corporate events, I travel anywhere — travel fees apply outside a 50-mile radius.
Portraits and headshots: 1-2 weeks. Weddings: 4-6 weeks. Real estate: 24-48 hours. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.
The Essential and Signature collections include digital files with a personal print release. The Luxe collection includes full commercial print rights. I also offer professional printing through my lab partners.
Weather happens — especially in Florida. We will reschedule at no extra cost, or we can embrace it. Some of my favorite photos have been shot in dramatic weather.
