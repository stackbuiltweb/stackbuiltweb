Our Story

Built on 18 years of trust.

From a two-man crew to Brevard County's most trusted roofing contractor. This is how we got here.

From the ground up.

In 2008, Jake Caldwell left a comfortable position at a national roofing franchise with one conviction: homeowners deserved better. Better materials. Better communication. Better results. He started Ironclad Roofing with a single truck, a small crew, and a handshake promise — every roof would be built as if it were his own.

Eighteen years later, that promise hasn't changed. What has changed is the scale. Ironclad now employs 35 skilled professionals, operates a fleet of 12 service vehicles, and has completed over 800 residential and commercial roofing projects across Brevard County. We've weathered the same storms our customers have — literally — and come out stronger every time.

We're not the cheapest option, and we don't try to be. We're the option you won't regret choosing. Every material is hand-selected. Every installation follows manufacturer specifications to the letter. Every project is backed by a 25-year workmanship warranty because we know the quality of what we build.
