What We Do

Roofing services built to last.

Every job gets the same attention to detail whether it is a single repair or a full tear-off. Here is what we do and what is included.

Full Roof Replacement

Complete tear-off and replacement with your choice of architectural shingles, tile, or metal. We remove the old roof down to the deck, inspect for damage, install new underlayment and flashing, and finish with a clean, warrantied roof. Most jobs completed in 1-3 days.

  • Old roof removal
  • Deck inspection
  • Underlayment
  • Flashing
  • Full clean-up
  • 25-year warranty
Emergency Storm Repair

Same-day response for hurricane and storm damage. We start with emergency tarping to stop active leaks, then provide full documentation with photos for your insurance claim. We work directly with your adjuster to get the job done right and paid for.

  • Same-day response
  • Emergency tarping
  • Insurance claim assistance
  • Full photo documentation
  • Direct adjuster coordination
Roof Inspections

Comprehensive 27-point inspection covering shingles, flashing, vents, gutters, underlayment, and structural integrity. You get a detailed written report with photos showing exactly what we found. Free for all Brevard County homeowners, no obligation.

  • 27-point checklist
  • Photo report
  • Structural assessment
  • Written findings
  • Free — no obligation
Gutter Installation

Seamless aluminum gutters custom-fabricated on site to fit your roofline. We install gutter guards to keep debris out and downspout extensions to direct water away from your foundation. Prevents costly water damage to your home and landscaping.

  • Seamless aluminum
  • Custom fabrication
  • Gutter guards
  • Downspout extensions
  • Foundation protection
Metal Roofing

Standing seam and corrugated metal roof installation built to outlast Florida weather. 50+ year lifespan, wind-rated to 140 MPH, with an energy-efficient reflective coating that lowers your cooling bill. The last roof you will ever need.

  • Standing seam or corrugated
  • 140 MPH wind rating
  • 50+ year lifespan
  • Energy-efficient coating
  • Manufacturer warranty
Commercial Flat Roofing

TPO, EPDM, and modified bitumen flat roof systems for commercial properties. Maintenance plans available to extend roof life and catch problems early. Serving warehouses, offices, and retail locations across Brevard County.

  • TPO / EPDM / modified bitumen
  • Maintenance plans
  • Commercial-grade
  • Code compliant
  • Minimal disruption
Our Story

Built on reputation. Backed by 18 years.

Founded in 2008 by Mike Torres, Ironclad Roofing started as a one-truck operation with a simple promise: do the job right, every time. Eighteen years later, we have replaced over 800 roofs across Brevard County.

We are still family-owned, still licensed and insured, and still answering the phone when a storm rolls through at midnight. Every crew member is background-checked, trained in-house, and held to the same standard Mike set on day one.
